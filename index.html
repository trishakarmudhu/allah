Customer wanted a 5' x 7' Mirror TV, so we delivered!

A customer recently reached out regarding the professional installation of a 5' x 7' mirror integrated with a TV for their fitness room. They specified a clear vision: no gimmicks or questionable solutions—everything had to be simple and reliable.

Given our extensive experience with Seura, particularly with outdoor TVs and Mirror TVs, we decided to collaborate with them for this project. Their expertise streamlined the process of determining the appropriate mount type, selecting the ideal TV location within the mirror, and drafting the necessary design schematics.

In addition to Seura's guidance, we incorporated some innovative elements to enhance the customer's experience. We enabled the ability to stream content to the TV and ensured that its audio effectively fills the fitness area. Also, we designed the installation so that servicing does not require removing the substantial 400lb unit from the wall. This ensures functionality: when the mirror is in use, it serves as a mirror, and when the TV is activated, it seamlessly appears in the upper left corner of the mirror.

Ultimately, the result has exceeded the customer's expectations, and they are extremely pleased with the installed solution and its added features.
